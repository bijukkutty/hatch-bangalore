About

Creatives, catalysts and patrons fail to connect, learn, co-create, and develop sustainable services and businesses. This is a fragmented and exploited ecosystem. It is failing to bring beauty and goodness to our society that it is capable of. We at ‘Let's create’ believe that this is a problem worth fixing. A networked inter-disciplinary collaboration platform to nurture creativity, transparency, innovation, co-creation & entrepreneurship to serve the common good is a right step to foster a thriving ecosystem.

We are a community of creatives, catalysts, patrons and entrepreneurs at the convergence of arts, design, media, science, technology and business to connect, learn, co-create, mentor and bring ideas to life.
